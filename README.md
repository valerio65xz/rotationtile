## вңҰ What is this?

**RotationTile** adds a single tile to your Android Quick Settings panel that toggles your screen between **portrait** and **landscape** вҖ” without fumbling through settings menus.

One tap. Done.

---

## вңҰ Features

- рҹ”„ &nbsp;**Instant toggle** between portrait and landscape
- рҹ“Ң &nbsp;**Locks orientation** вҖ” disables auto-rotate so it stays where you put it
- рҹҺӣпёҸ &nbsp;**Quick Settings tile** вҖ” lives right in your notification shade
- рҹӘ¶ &nbsp;**Featherweight** вҖ” no background services, no battery drain, no bloat
- рҹ”’ &nbsp;**One permission** вҖ” only needs `WRITE_SETTINGS` to change rotation

## вңҰ Setup (one-time)

> The app needs `WRITE_SETTINGS` to change system rotation. This is a one-time step.

1. **Open the app** from your launcher вҖ” a dialog will appear
2. Tap **"Open Settings"** and enable the toggle for *Rotation Tile*
3. Pull down your notification shade and tap the **pencil / edit** icon
4. Find **"Rotate Screen"** and drag it into your tiles
5. Done вҖ” tap the tile anytime to toggle orientation вң“

